# Bangalore Interior Design Firms - Discovery/Consultation Page Analysis

## Executive Summary
This research document analyzes the discovery and consultation page approaches of six major Bangalore-based interior design firms. The analysis covers data collection methods, quiz/questionnaire structures, UX patterns, and call-to-action strategies.

## Detailed Analysis by Category

### 1. **Data Collection Approach**

#### Minimalist Approach
- **HomeLane**: 5 fields (Name, Email, Mobile, Pincode, WhatsApp checkbox)
- **Design Café**: 5-6 fields (Property type, Location, Phone, Email, WhatsApp opt-in)
- **Interia Studio**: No online form; phone/email only

#### Comprehensive Approach
- **Livspace**: Multiple data points collected across calculator tools (dimensions, style, budget, family details, floor plans)
- **Bonito Designs**: 6 key data points across 2-step form (Location, pincode, unit type, budget range, possession timeline, neighborhood)

### 2. **Quiz/Questionnaire Structure**

**Key Finding: None of these firms use a traditional multi-question quiz format for initial discovery.**

Instead, they employ:
- **Calculators** (Livspace): Self-serve price estimation tools
- **Structured Forms** (Bonito, Design Café, HomeLane): Sequential data collection
- **Direct Consultation** (Interia): Phone/email-based discovery

### 3. **Contact Information Timing**

| **After property details** | Bonito Designs |

## Key Insights & Recommendations

### Best Practices Observed

1. **Upfront Contact vs. Progressive Engagement**
   - Early contact (HomeLane, Design Café): Better lead capture, faster response
   - Progressive form (Bonito): Better qualification, reduces noise

2. **Multi-Calculator Approach (Livspace)**
   - Enables self-serve discovery
   - Reduces consultation time for informed clients
   - Builds confidence through transparency

3. **WhatsApp Integration**
   - Mobile-first communication preference
   - Design Café, HomeLane leverage it effectively

4. **3D Visualization (HomeLane)**
   - Strong differentiator for technical visualization
   - Requires designer expertise and time investment
   - Desktop-optimized experience indicates B2B/professional focus

5. **Celebrity Positioning (Bonito)**
   - Clear brand differentiation
   - Attracts design-conscious affluent segment

6. **Designer Network Scale (Design Café)**
   - 400+ designers messaging
   - Builds trust and capacity assurance

### Missing Patterns Across Industry

- **Image-based style quizzes**: None of the firms currently use image galleries or mood board selection quizzes for initial discovery
- **Video consultations ease**: HomeLane explicitly recommends desktop; others don't highlight video options
- **Detailed family/lifestyle pre-assessment**: Most defer this to post-contact consultant conversations
- **Interactive room/space builders**: No pre-consultation space planning tools found
- **Progress timelines in initial form**: Budget and timeline captured at different stages
